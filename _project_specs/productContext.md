# EstimaPro - Product Context

## Problem Statement
Software development teams face multiple obstacles when performing estimations:

1. **Cognitive Biases:** Individual biases (anchoring, leader influence) affect estimation accuracy
2. **Group Dynamics:** Negative group dynamics can contaminate the estimation process
3. **Fear of Judgment:** Team members may not contribute their perspective due to social pressure
4. **Coordination Challenges:** Coordinating multiple estimation rounds across different time zones and remote/hybrid work
5. **Manual Overhead:** Excessive time spent on administrative tasks (collection, consolidation, analysis)
6. **Lack of Traceability:** Incomplete historical records hinder audits and organizational learning
7. **Convergence Difficulty:** No objective way to determine when sufficient consensus has been reached

## Solution Overview
EstimaPro addresses these problems through:

- **Structured Process:** Iterative rounds with independent, anonymous estimation submissions
- **Multiple Methods:** Wideband Delphi, Planning Poker, Three-Point (PERT) selection
- **Automated Analysis:** Statistical calculations (mean, median, std dev, variance, CV, IQR)
- **Visual Feedback:** Histograms, box plots, evolution charts, comparative views
- **Asynchronous Communication:** Comment system for justifications without compromising anonymity
- **Convergence Detection:** Automatic evaluation of consensus level with configurable thresholds
- **Complete Traceability:** Historical record of all rounds, estimates, justifications, and decisions
